CORONA

Virus Disease (COVID-19)

Pray for Coronavirus victims.The virus was first reported in Wuhan,

Hubei, China on 17th November 2019, and on 11 March 2020, the

World Health Organisation [WHO] declared the

outbreak a pandemic.

– PREVENTION –

01

HandWashing

Wash your hands frequently throughout the day, especially before meals.

You should spend at least 10-20 seconds washing.

The CDC has more information about the proper handwashing technique.

02

Use Mask

Whether or not you are feeling sick or have COVID-19 symptoms, there is evidence that if you have the virus, you may transmit it.

A mask is meant to protect others and yourself.

The mask is not recommended for children younger than 2 years!

03

Be Cautious On What You Touch

Avoid grasing shared surfaces such as door handles, gas pumps, grocery carts etc.

You can use your knuckle to touch things like light switches and elevator buttons in public locations.

At the gas station, hold the gasoline dispenser with a paper towel or disposable gloves.

04

Social Distancing

Maintain a distance of 6 feet between yourself and others.

05

Wash Clothes

Much like our bodies,germs also like to cling to our clothes.

Your clothes should be clean and free of any bacteria or virus.

06

Use Hand Sanitizer

Keep a bottle of hand sanitizer that is greater than 60% alcohol at home and in your car.

Use the sanitizer after touching any public object.

Additionally, use disinfectant wipes at stores whenever they are available.
